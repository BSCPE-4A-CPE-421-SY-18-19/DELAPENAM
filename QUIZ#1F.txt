QUIZ#1: FINAL TERM
CPE 421- TROUBLESHOOTING OF COMPUTER SYSTEMS


Why the P5GC ASUS or 1333 Intel did not boot to BIOS?


Possible Causes:

---> Faulty RAM (something wrong related to RAM)
	-Try transferring to another slot or replace it.

---> Faulting PCI Cards

---> Misplaced front panel connectors

---> Missing power supply cables
	-Check the 24-pin ATX cable and the 4-pin or 8-pin CPU connector.

---> The CPU is dead.

---> The PSU not working properly.
